2020 Glassdoor Data Analyst Job Listings in NY State

by Kirsten Pevidal

Context

Looking to start a career in Data Analytics but have no idea what sector or industry will be on-demand? As someone who wants to become a data analyst in NY, I am curious about the job prospect in data analytics here in NYC.

It would be nice if there is some sort of "guide" that can help you decide which industries are thriving during the COVID19 pandemic and how much you are expected to get paid for. So, I decided to try making one as my first Data Analytics projects!

The Dataset

I found a dataset in Kaggle that contains scraped data of all Data Analyst Jobs published in Glassdoor for the year 2020. This dataset is pretty interesting as it was scraped to help job seekers during the COVID19 Pandemic.

Anyways, this dataset contains 2253 job listings from glassdoor. I cleaned the data to only include the 345 job listings from NY State. I also manually cleaned some of the data I gathered and splitted some of the columns in order to have the following:

  • Job Title
  • Company Name
  • Minimum, Median, Maximum Salary (in USD)
  • Rating (from 1-5)
  • Company Size
  • Industry
  • Sector

Ratings

The ratings may not be up-to-date but it is still interesting to see how companies in the top industries do. Here, we can see that the Staffing & Outsourcing industry has the highest mean rating.

Overall, the most important information here is the average rating.
Keep in mind: Companies constantly plant positive reviews of their own company to improve overall ratings.

Locations

90% of the job listings are in Manhattan (New York, NY). I do not have the data to determine which listings offers WFH (work-from-home). That will be a good thing to consider for further studies since that 2020 is the year of WFH.
